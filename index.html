Frequently Asked Questions about Quality Engineering

Common questions about our Quality Engineering approach, methodologies, and tools.

What is the difference between QA and Quality Engineering (QE)?

QA (Quality Assurance) is a defensive, post-development inspection focused on defect detection. QE (Quality Engineering) is a proactive, code-centric discipline that embeds quality and architectural integrity from the product's conception, driving measurable business outcomes.

Why do you specifically mandate Playwright and Postman?

These are best-in-class, globally competitive methodologies. Playwright provides fast, reliable E2E automation for multi-browser testing, while Postman is crucial for robust backend API validation, where much of a SaaS application's critical logic resides.

What are "Shift-Left" and "Shift-Right" testing?

They are principles of Continuous Testing. Shift-Left means testing early and often during development (catching defects when cheapest to fix). Shift-Right involves continuous monitoring and validation post-deployment to ensure quality under real-world production loads.

Where is Rofane Consulting officially based?

Rofane Consulting (Pty) Ltd is officially based in Gauteng, South Africa, which serves as the economic and technological hub of the nation, providing immediate alignment with our high-value client base.

How does Rofane ensure data integrity?

We integrate deep database knowledge via SQL proficiency directly into our automation strategy, allowing us to validate the absolute integrity of underlying data structures, which is critical for preventing significant financial defects in transactional systems.
