Strategic IT Talent Acquisition Offerings

At Rofane Consulting, our recruitment services extend beyond traditional staffing. We specialize in sourcing technical expertise, cultural fit, and domain knowledge to achieve successful project outcomes. Our recruitment process is founded on deep industry insights, extensive networks, and a commitment to matching the right talent with the right opportunities.

What Makes Our Talent Acquisition Different

Technical Understanding

Our consultants possess practical experience in IT delivery. We comprehend the technologies, methodologies, and tools relevant to each role, allowing us to assess candidates accurately beyond resume evaluations.

Domain Expertise

With extensive experience in banking, financial services, and enterprise IT, we match candidates who bring relevant domain knowledge to accelerate productivity and reduce learning curves.

Rigorous Vetting Process

Our multi-stage screening includes:

  • Initial skills and experience assessment
  • Expert interviews
  • Tool and technology proficiency verification
  • Domain knowledge assessment
  • Reference and credential verification
  • Cultural fit evaluation

Extensive Network

Our years of building relationships give us access to:

  • Active job seekers
  • Passive candidates (not actively looking but open to opportunities)
  • Alumni from previous placements
  • Graduates from our Training Academy
  • Industry referrals and recommendations

Quality Guarantee

We stand behind our placements with:

  • Replacement guarantees for unsuccessful hires
  • Defined probation period support
  • Performance check-ins
  • Issue resolution assistance
  • Long-term relationship building
